About Mufti Gallery

Established in 1953 by Ahmed Elmufti, Mufti Gallery began as a small family shop focused on local antiques and handcrafted pieces. The gallery continues to operate today under the management of Ibrahim Elmufti. Over the decades we have curated a wide variety of antiques, souvenirs, handcrafts, and vintage goods sourced locally and regionally.

History

From our modest beginnings in 1953 to the present day, Mufti Gallery has remained dedicated to preserving and sharing unique pieces with collectors and interior lovers alike. Each item is inspected and described, and many items come with a story that connects them to the past.
